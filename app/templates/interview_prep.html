Interview Preparation Checklist

Follow this checklist to maximize your chances of landing the offer.

Master the STAR Technique

Situation, Task, Action, Result — structure all behavioral answers this way.

Review Top 50 DSA Patterns

Arrays, Trees, Graphs, Dynamic Programming, Sliding Window, Two Pointers.

Practice System Design

Load Balancers, Caching, Database Sharding, Message Queues, CDNs.

Prepare 2-Minute Self-Intro

Craft a concise elevator pitch: background, skills, why you're a fit.

Research the Company

Study recent products, culture, values, leadership, and news articles.

Do a Mock Interview

Use Pramp, Interviewing.io, or practice with a peer for real feedback.

Review SQL & Database Concepts

JOINs, GROUP BY, window functions, indexing, normalization.

Prepare Questions for Interviewer

Ask about team culture, tech stack, growth paths, day-to-day responsibilities.

Test Your Virtual Setup

Camera, microphone, internet stability, background, lighting for video calls.

Know Your Resume Inside Out

Be ready to discuss every project, skill, and experience on your resume.

Practice Time Management

Solve medium problems in 20-25 min. Use a timer during practice sessions.

Review OOP & Design Patterns

SOLID principles, Factory, Observer, Strategy, Singleton patterns.
